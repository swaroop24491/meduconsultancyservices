About Us

Our Story

Medu Consultancy was founded by Medu Thirumaleshwara Bhat in 2001. He has 35 years of experience working with the Employee Provident Fund Organization (EPFO). Medu Consultancy initially started as a computer training center. However, seeing a growing need for expert guidance in Provident Fund (PF) and Employee State Insurance (ESI) compliance, we pivoted to focus on these services. we are proud to have become a trusted name in EPF and ESI consultancy.

Our Journey

What began as a local service in the coastal towns of Mangalore, Udupi, Puttur,Bantwal, Kundapura, and Gokarna, gradually expanded to serve businesses throughout Karnataka. Today, we cater to major cities such as Bangalore, Mysore, Hubballi, Hassan, Sakleshpur, Davanagere, Sirsi, Madikeri and beyond, providing comprehensive, reliable, and expert solutions to help businesses stay compliant with EPF and ESI regulations.

Leadership

At the helm of our company is Medu Thirumaleshwara Bhat, the CEO, whose leadership and vision have guided Medu Consultancy to success. With decades of experience and a passion for helping businesses navigate the complexities of EPF and ESI compliance, his dedication has been the driving force behind our continued growth.

Our Mission

At Medu Consultancy, our mission is to simplify the complexities of EPF and ESI compliance for businesses, ensuring seamless operations and full regulatory compliance. With personalized support, expert knowledge, and over 20 years of experience, we are here to empower your business to thrive while we handle your compliance needs.
