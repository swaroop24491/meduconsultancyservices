Frequently Asked Questions

EPF (Employees' Provident Fund) is a government-mandated savings scheme for employees, where both employers and employees contribute. It helps provide financial security to employees after retirement. As a business, adhering to EPF regulations ensures legal compliance and contributes to your employees' welfare.

We specialize in providing end-to-end EPF compliance services, from registration and UAN generation to monthly filings, audits, and liaising with the EPF department. Our expertise helps small businesses stay compliant without the hassle.

Medu Consultancy Services will handle your EPF registration process, ensuring all paperwork is completed accurately. We register your business with EPFO and take care of the employee registration and UAN generation.

UAN is a unique number assigned to each employee to consolidate their EPF accounts. We handle UAN generation for your employees, making it easier for them to access their EPF balance and manage transfers when switching jobs.

Both the employer and employee contribute to EPF, with the employer contributing an equal percentage of the employee's basic salary. As an employer, we help ensure timely and accurate contributions.

Yes, non-compliance can lead to penalties, including fines and interest on unpaid contributions. Medu Consultancy Services ensures that your business avoids such penalties by managing timely filings and updates.

EPF returns need to be filed monthly. We ensure all required documentation is submitted on time, taking the burden off your team.

If an employee leaves your business, we help guide them through the process of transferring their EPF or withdrawing the balance, ensuring smooth and hassle-free transactions.

For EPF registration, we’ll need your business’s PAN, GST number (if applicable), and employee details like Aadhaar, PAN, and bank account information. Our team will assist you in collecting and submitting these documents.

We assist in collecting and updating KYC details for your employees to ensure they are in line with EPF requirements. This includes updating details like Aadhaar, PAN, and bank account information.

Medu Consultancy Services will guide you in maintaining accurate records and ensure you are prepared for any inspections from the EPF department. We will also assist with submitting any required documentation.
