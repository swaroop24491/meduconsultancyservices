Businesses with more than 20 employees are required to register for EPF and more than 10 employees are required to register for ESI. However, smaller businesses may also choose to opt in based on their requirements.

We assist with the complete registration process for both EPF and ESI, ensuring compliance with all necessary documentation and requirements.

EPF provides financial security for employees after retirement and covers contributions for retirement, pension, and insurance benefits.

EPF returns need to be filed monthly, covering both the employer's and employee's contributions to the fund.

Documents like PAN, business registration proof, employee details, and bank account information are required for EPF registration.

ESI provides medical benefits, maternity benefits, sickness benefits, disability benefits, and pensions for employees.

We assist with the complete ESI registration process, ensuring that your business meets all eligibility requirements.

As per current laws, the employer contributes 3.25%, and the employee contributes 0.75% of the employee's gross salary towards ESI.
